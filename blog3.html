Vegan Smoothie Ideas



Chia, Berry, and Cardamom Vegan Smoothie


Chia seeds give this vegan smoothie its thick texture, while cardamom adds a distinct sweetness. We use a mix of raspberries and strawberries here, but feel free to experiment with your favorite fruits.

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups almond milk
3 tablespoons chia seeds
1 cup frozen raspberries
1/2 cup frozen strawberries
1 teaspoon ground cardamom

Directions

1. In a small bowl, mix together 1 cup almond milk with chia seeds. Let sit at least 20 minutes and up to 1 hour, until chia expands and the texture becomes pudding-like.
2.Transfer chia mixture to blender and add remaining 1/2 cup almond milk, frozen berries, and cardamom.
3. Puree ingredients in a blender until smooth.



Spinach, Avocado, and Cucumber Vegan Smoothie


Cucumber lends its cooling flavor to this green vegan smoothie, which packs in 2 cups of antioxidant-rich baby spinach.

Ingredients

1/2 cup almond milk
2 big handfuls baby spinach
1/2 small cucumber
1/2 avocado
Juice of 1 lemon

Directions

Puree ingredients in a blender until smooth.



Apple Pie Vegan Smoothie


This liquid comfort food tastes like dessert (just like our Apple Pie Smoothie Bowl), but it's actually full of nutrition. The fiber from the oats and apple will hold you over until your next meal, while the ice keeps the vegan smoothie satisfyingly thick without adding calories.

Ingredients

1/2 cup organic rolled oats, soaked overnight in water and drained of excess liquid
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1 tablespoon almond butter
1/2 apple, diced
1/2 cup unsweetened coconut milk
1 cup ice cubes
1/2 cup water

Directions

Puree ingredients in a blender until smooth.
